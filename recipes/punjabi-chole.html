Punjabi Chole

Description

A spicy and tangy chickpea curry made with boiled chole (white chickpeas), bold masala base, and aromatic Indian spices. Best enjoyed with bhature or rice.

Ingredients

  • White chickpeas (chole) – 1 cup
  • Onion – 1 (finely chopped)
  • Tomatoes – 2 (pureed)
  • Ginger-garlic paste – 1 tbsp
  • Oil – 2 tbsp
  • Cumin seeds – 1 tsp
  • Chole masala – 1 tsp
  • Red chili powder – 1 tsp
  • Turmeric – 1/2 tsp
  • Coriander powder – 1 tsp
  • Garam masala – 1/2 tsp
  • Tea bag (optional, for dark color) – 1
  • Salt – to taste
  • Coriander leaves, lemon wedges – for garnish

Steps

  1. Soak chickpeas overnight.
  2. Pressure cook with salt and a tea bag for 5–6 whistles.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds.
  4. Sauté onions till golden.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste, cook for 30 seconds.
  6. Add tomato puree, chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, salt.
  7. Cook till oil separates.
  8. Add boiled chole with 1 cup water.
  9. Simmer on low for 15–20 mins.
  10. Slightly mash some chole for thick gravy.
  11. Add garam masala and stir.
  12. Garnish with coriander and lemon.
  13. Serve with bhature, puri, or rice.
