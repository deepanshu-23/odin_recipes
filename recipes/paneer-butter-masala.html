Paneer Butter Masala

Description

A restaurant-style creamy curry made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese), tomato-cashew gravy, and loads of butter. Mildly sweet and rich in flavor.

Ingredients

  • Paneer – 200g (cubed)
  • Onion – 1 (chopped)
  • Tomatoes – 2 (pureed) or 1 cup tomato puree
  • Ginger-garlic paste – 1 tbsp
  • Butter – 2 tbsp
  • Fresh cream – 1/4 cup
  • Red chili powder – 1 tsp
  • Turmeric – 1/2 tsp
  • Garam masala – 1 tsp
  • Kasuri methi – 1 tsp
  • Sugar – 1 tsp
  • Salt – to taste
  • Water – as needed

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tbsp butter + 1 tbsp oil in a pan.
  2. Sauté onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste; cook for 30 seconds.
  4. Add tomato puree, chili powder, turmeric, salt. Cook till oil separates.
  5. Cool and blend this to a smooth paste.
  6. Reheat the paste with 1 tbsp butter in the pan.
  7. Add water to adjust gravy consistency.
  8. Add paneer cubes and cook for 5 minutes.
  9. Add cream, sugar, garam masala, and kasuri methi. Mix well.
  10. Simmer for 2–3 mins and turn off heat.
  11. Garnish with cream and coriander.
  12. Serve hot with naan, paratha, or pulao.
